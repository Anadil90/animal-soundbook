About Animal Soundbook

This website has been created with children in mind. As a parent himself, the developer feels the need to put the duties of being a father forward. He has been looking for ways to entice his child into learning on a more effective scale, and is why this website was made. All of the materials seen on this website is part of a ongoing personal research to improve the learning efficacy in small children. Here you, the user, can be a part of this process and help to perhaps further develop an online solution that may play a big part in the learning of children. Therefore, I thank you a lot for visting this website.
